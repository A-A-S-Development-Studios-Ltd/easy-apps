How we do it

Simple Development Process

Phase 1.
Design

Our journey starts by narrowing down exactly what the app needs to do and specifics for how it will be used. We'll discuss and list all the features, screen layouts, main functions and many other specific app requirements.

Then we start designing a visual mockup or prototype of your app, allowing you to see how your app should look once it is finished. This can include images of each screen with some sample content on it, or videos showing the app being used and the expected outcomes with each action.

Often after seeing the prototype in action, it can inspire new functions or layouts which we would then adjust the design and development scope to include them.

Phase 2.
Develop

With all the details we need, now we start building the app itself. This process can be timely, depending of course on the complexity of your app, so we provide you with regular progress updates and ensure reasonable timelines for deliverables are met.

You will know what we are working on and how long each step took with detailed development logs and weekly updates.

This phase can also include setting up any other servers or hardware required, this can often include working directly with you to ensure that these services, and related credentials are securely set up so you will retain full administrative access over the accounts related to your app.

Phase 3.
Deploy

Lastly we will deploy your app so it is fully available for beta testing by you or your team to ensure everything is running smoothly. Once everything is completed we make sure your app is published and available for commercial use.

After this ship has sailed and your app is deployed, ongoing maintenance services are often required to keep your servers secure, your apps compatible with the next generation of devices, or evolving platforms requiring additions to maintain compliance with best practises for security and customer protection.

We offer services to help keep you running long into the future.
