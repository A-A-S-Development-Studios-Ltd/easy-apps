Individually Crafted Plans

The same way each app we develop will be unique to fit that customers specific needs, our pricing plans are able to be tailored to each individual customer. This means you will have the flexibility to ensure compliance with any potential requirements regarding payment terms or schedules.

Our standard process for creating a full quote is established during the prototyping process, so the first phase of our contract is only for the prototype design and making detailed feature specification lists. Upon completion of the prototype we then evaluate it and offer the full quote for the development of the app and all costs related to its upkeep and deployment. This can include quotes for how much ongoing server expenses could cost, or any other publishing licence costs.

Our goal is always to provide quality development services in an easy and agreeable manner, so if you have any questions about our payment terms or would like to get a quote yourself, send us a message to schedule your free consultation.
